What is Server AREX

Server AREX is an opensource project which merges a variety of technological tools with an objective to achieve connectivity to NASA's server open data. Connectivity will be established through the use of a Web API that makes available to the public the "Picture of the Day" preview and download from NASA's server.

Purpose

Exploration and freedom of speech is a human right. We all are part of a whole, 195 countries worldwide, with 7.594 billion people living and breathing on planet earth. Server AREX aims to bring awareness and inspiration to people from around the globe to come up with creative and innovative ideas against COVID-19.

Call to Action

World Wide Web, we have achieved marvelous things since the invention of ARPANET. If you are a PhD student doing a theses relevant to COVID-19, do not hesitate to contact us at server-arex@gmail.com to host and share your research on the Internet for free.
